# Swiggy-Food-Delivery-Sales-Dashboard-Excel-Data-Analysis-
This project presents an interactive Swiggy Food Delivery Dashboard created using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard analyzes food delivery sales performance, customer ratings, order trends, and geographic distribution to generate useful business insights.

## Tools & Technologies Used
Microsoft Excel – Data analysis and dashboard creation ,
Pivot Tables – Data aggregation and analysis  ,
Charts & Graphs – Data visualization ,
Slicers – Interactive filtering .

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains food delivery order information including:-
Order Date ,
State ,
City ,
Restaurant Name ,
Food Category ,
Food Type (Veg / Non-Veg) ,
Order Value ,
Rating ,
Rating Count ,
Total Orders .

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Sales: ₹53.01M ,
Average Rating: 4.34 ,
Average Order Value: ₹268.51 ,
Rating Count: 5.59M ,
Total Orders: 197.43K .

## Dashboard Featurs
Sales Performance :-

Sales distribution by food type (Veg vs Non-Veg)

## Geographic Analysis
Sales distribution across Indian states ,
Top cities contributing to sales .

## Customer Feedback 
Average customer ratings ,
Total rating count analysis .

## Restaurant Insights 
Sales comparison across restaurants ,
Category-wise product analysis .

## Interactive Filters
The dashboard includes slicers for:
Month ,
Category ,
Restaurant Name ,
These filters allow dynamic exploration of the data.

## Key Insights
Non-vegetarian food items contribute the majority of sales ,
Saturday shows the highest daily sales ,
Cities like Bengaluru, Lucknow, and Hyderabad generate the highest revenue,
Sales remain consistently strong throughout the year with slight monthly fluctuations .
